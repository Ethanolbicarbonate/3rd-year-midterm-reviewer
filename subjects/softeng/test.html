  1. Scenario 1: A web application is monitored for 50 hours. During this time, it experiences three failures. The first failure occurs after 15 hours of uptime and takes 2 hours to repair. The second failure occurs after 20 more hours of uptime and takes 3 hours to repair. The third failure occurs after 8 more hours of uptime and takes 2 hours to repair.

    What is the total operational time (uptime)?
    Answer: 15 + 20 + 8 = 43 hours
  2. (Scenario 1 cont.) What is the total maintenance time (downtime)?
    Answer: 2 + 3 + 2 = 7 hours

  8. Scenario 2: An IT department deploys 500 new, non-repairable hard drives. After 1,000,000 hours of total combined operation, all 500 have failed. What is the Mean Time To Failure (MTTF) for these hard drives?
    Answer: MTTF = Total Hours / Total # of Units = 1,000,000 / 500 = 2000 hours
  9. Scenario 3: A system has a known Availability of 98% (0.98) and an MTTR of 2 hours. What is the MTBF of this system?
    Answer: 
    A = MTBF / (MTBF + MTTR)
0.98 = MTBF / (MTBF + 2)
0.98 * (MTBF + 2) = MTBF
0.98 * MTBF + 1.96 = MTBF
1.96 = MTBF - 0.98 * MTBF
1.96 = 0.02 * MTBF
MTBF = 1.96 / 0.02 = 98 hours
